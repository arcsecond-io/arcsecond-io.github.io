Explore

Night Explorer (formerly known as iObserve) is the best pro tool for preparing astronomical observations.

Plan

Night Plans are a whole new observations planner, with custom rule sets. Create linear sequences of targets, manually ... or automatically!

Observe

Record your observing nights inside Night Logs, and easily associate data to each observation.

Store

Store data in our industry-grade secured AWS Cloud Storage, or attach your external storages to Arcsecond.

Distribute

Group your datasets into Data Packages, and distribute your data and work to astronomers, or your visiting observers.

Observatory Portals

All the above, with unlimited members, easy-to-manage 5 levels of membership, very modular portals. Pick up only what you need.
